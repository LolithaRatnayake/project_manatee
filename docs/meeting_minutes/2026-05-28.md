### Key Lessons & Mindset Takeaways

- **The Dual-Core of Architecture:** An Enterprise Architect does not just design systems; they must balance two distinct modes of operation:
- _Tactical Engagement:_ Solving immediate, complex problems and engaging stakeholders effectively.
- _Strategic Overview:_ Maintaining a holistic, high-level product overview to ensure individual solutions align with the broader corporate ecosystem.

- **The Power of 'Why':** Moving from a senior engineer to an architect requires shifting from _how_ to build something to _why_ it should be built. Interrogating the "Why" ensures that technology choices are driven by business capabilities and long-term strategy rather than technical convenience or trends.
